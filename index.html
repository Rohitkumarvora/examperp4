Core Project Management Methodologies

Predictive (Waterfall)

A traditional, linear approach where project stages are completed sequentially. Best for projects with well-defined requirements and a stable scope.

Agile

An iterative approach focused on flexibility and customer feedback. Work is broken into small increments (sprints). Ideal for projects where requirements are expected to change.

Hybrid

A combination of predictive and agile methods. This approach uses the stability of predictive planning for some phases and the flexibility of agile for others.
